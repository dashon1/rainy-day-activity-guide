Pro Tips & Activity Variations

Extending Activities
1. Add a "create your own version" challenge
2. Time trials add excitement
3. Turn solo activities into teams
4. Add scoring for competitive kids
5. Record and review (great for shy kids)
Managing Energy
* High-energy activities first
* Quiet activities after lunch
* Rotate between active and calm
* Build in snack breaks
Age-Appropriate Adjustments
* Simplify for younger children
* Add complexity for older kids
* Pair young with old for mentoring
* Mix age groups for cooperation
Bad Weather Backups
* Always have 3 activities ready
* Rotate favorites every few months
* Keep materials in a "rainy day box"
* Plan based on child mood
BONUS: 5 Quick Transition Activities
* "Would You Rather" game (5 min)
* Storytelling chain (10 min)
* Silent theater (10 min)
* Category race (5 min)
* Draw it / Act it out (15 min)
